Kesavananda Bharati v. State of Kerala (1973)

Citation: AIR 1973 SC 1461

Facts:

The petitioner challenged the 24th, 25th, and 29th Amendments...

Issue:

Whether Parliament had unlimited power to amend the Constitution.

Judgment:

The Supreme Court held that Parliament can amend the Constitution but not alter its basic structure.
