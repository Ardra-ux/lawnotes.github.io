Transfer of Property Act, 1882

Overview

Property law regulates the transfer of property between living persons, including sale, mortgage, lease, and gift.

Key Provisions

  • Sec. 5 – Transfer of Property defined.
  • Sec. 54 – Sale of immovable property.
  • Sec. 58 – Mortgage.
  • Sec. 105 – Lease.
  • Sec. 122 – Gift.

Landmark Cases

  • Ram Gopal v. Nand Lal – Rights of transferee.
  • K. Balakrishnan v. K. Kamalam – Validity of gift deeds.
