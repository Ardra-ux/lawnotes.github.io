Family Law (India)

Overview

Family law covers marriage, divorce, adoption, guardianship, succession, and inheritance, governed by different personal laws.

Key Legislations

  • Hindu Marriage Act, 1955
  • Hindu Succession Act, 1956
  • Special Marriage Act, 1954
  • Muslim Personal Law (Shariat), 1937

Landmark Cases

  • Sarla Mudgal v. Union of India – Bigamy & need for uniform civil code.
  • Shayara Bano v. Union of India – Triple Talaq unconstitutional.
