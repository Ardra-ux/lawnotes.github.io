Civil Procedure Code, 1908

Overview

CPC provides the procedure for adjudicating civil rights in courts. It ensures fair trial and justice in civil matters.

Key Parts

  • Jurisdiction – Territorial & pecuniary limits.
  • Pleadings – Plaint & Written Statement.
  • Interlocutory Proceedings – Temporary injunctions, interim orders.
  • Appeals & Revisions
  • Execution of Decrees

Landmark Cases

  • Sangram Singh v. Election Tribunal – CPC should be interpreted to advance justice.
  • Kedarnath v. Prahlad Rai – Res judicata principle.
