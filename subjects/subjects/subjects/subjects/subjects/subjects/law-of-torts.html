Law of Torts

Overview

Tort law deals with civil wrongs where the remedy is damages. It protects rights such as safety, property, reputation, and personal liberty.

Main Torts

  • Negligence
  • Defamation
  • Nuisance
  • Trespass
  • Strict & Absolute Liability

Landmark Cases

  • Rylands v. Fletcher – Rule of Strict Liability.
  • M.C. Mehta v. Union of India – Absolute Liability in India.
