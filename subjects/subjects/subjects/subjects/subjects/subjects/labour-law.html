Labour & Industrial Law (India)

Overview

Labour law regulates the relationship between employers, workers, and trade unions, ensuring fair treatment and welfare.

Important Legislations

  • Industrial Disputes Act, 1947
  • Factories Act, 1948
  • Minimum Wages Act, 1948
  • Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948
  • Code on Wages, 2019 (new labour codes)

Landmark Cases

  • Bandhua Mukti Morcha v. Union of India – Right against bonded labour.
  • Workmen of Dimakuchi Tea Estate v. Management – Definition of "workman".
