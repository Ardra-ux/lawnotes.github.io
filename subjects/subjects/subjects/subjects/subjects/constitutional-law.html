Constitutional Law (India)

Overview

Constitutional Law deals with the framework of government, distribution of powers, and fundamental rights of citizens.

Key Parts

  • Preamble
  • Fundamental Rights (Part III)
  • Directive Principles of State Policy (Part IV)
  • Fundamental Duties (Part IVA)
  • Union and State Government powers

Landmark Cases

  • Keshavananda Bharati v. State of Kerala – Basic Structure Doctrine.
  • Maneka Gandhi v. Union of India – Expansive interpretation of Art. 21.
