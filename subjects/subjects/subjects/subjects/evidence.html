Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam, 2023

Overview

The Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam, 2023 is the new law that consolidates and repeals the Indian Evidence Act, 1872. It aims to streamline and modernize the rules of evidence in Indian courts, with a significant focus on digital and electronic evidence.
