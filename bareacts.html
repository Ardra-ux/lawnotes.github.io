Bare Acts

Quick access to important Indian Bare Acts, including both the original and the newly enacted laws of 2023.

Indian Penal Code, 1860 (IPC)

The official criminal code of India, now repealed.

Replaced by: Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023
Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023 (BNS)

The new comprehensive law to replace the Indian Penal Code.

In Effect
Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (CrPC)

The main legislation on procedure for administration of substantive criminal law in India.

Replaced by: Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023
Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (CPC)

The procedural law governing civil litigation in India.
Indian Evidence Act, 1872

The law governing the admissibility of evidence in Indian courts, now repealed.

Replaced by: Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam, 2023
